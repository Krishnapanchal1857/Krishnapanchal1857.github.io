Our Story

Built by Students, For Students

SAKSHAR (सक्षर) means "educated" or "literate" in Hindi. We built this platform because we saw too many students struggling to find good resources — spending money on books they could access for free, or failing exams simply because they couldn't find past papers.

We are a team of students and educators who believe quality education should not depend on financial background. Every student deserves equal access — whether from Mumbai, Nagpur, or a small town in rural Maharashtra.

SAKSHAR brings textbooks, reference materials, notes, and previous year papers into one free, organized, department-wise platform. And with SMRITI AI, every student now has a personal AI tutor available 24/7.
